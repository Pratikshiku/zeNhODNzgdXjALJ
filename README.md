## Medical Registration Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/4f073dff1c124190875004b182e4d737/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Functional Description
The system is divided into (roles): doctor users, billing staff users, registration staff users, administrators.

**Doctor User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Homepage
3. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
4. Case Management
5. Registration Management
6. Patient Management
7. Outpatient Management
8. Physical Examination Management
9. Medicine Management


**Billing Staff User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Homepage
3. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
4. Case Management
5. Registration Management
6. Patient Management
7. Outpatient Management
8. Physical Examination Management
9. Medicine Management
10. Doctor Management


**Registration Staff User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Homepage
3. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
4. Case Management
5. Registration Management
6. Patient Management
7. Outpatient Management
8. Physical Examination Management
9. Medicine Management

**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Homepage
3. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
4. Basic Data Management (department management, medicine type management, position management)
5. Case Management
6. Registration Management
7. Registration Staff Management
8. Billing Staff Management
9. Patient Management
10. Outpatient Management
11. Physical Examination Management
12. Medicine Management
13. Doctor Management
